About Us

YOU

The Next Generation

Here, YOU Lead. At times where beauty standards need to be defeated, we make modeling, acting, content creation for everyone.

We believe in a safe space and inclusivity where modeling is a community and not an industry.

A community of self confident women and men looking for a way to be able to express themselves through art and creation.

Welcome to "You," the revolutionary space for a community dedicated to encourage your personal and professional growth.

At "You," our focus is solely on you.

We believe in your potential and are committed to providing you with the tools, inspiration, and community to help you become your best self, regardless of your background orbeliefs

Objective

In a world where cameras and lights are part of our daily lives, the world decided to tell us who are the voices that can be heard.

We are here to defeat these misconceptions, and let the world know YOU are here to be seen and heard.

While we work together on self-acknowledgement and empowerment, we help you discover your voice, enhance your techniques, and show what is already inside of you.

We do not create YOU, or labe YOU, we just guide YOU, to show YOU.

Orchestrated by the the experienced model and content creator that defeated many beauty standards, Rita Samaha, this workshop is a passionate ode to every individual, regardless of shape, size, ethnicity,or background.

Mission

To equip YOU with the best tools to create magic, Our mission is to empower YOU by creating opportunities, delivering knowledge, and cultivating a supportive community.

We are here to inspire, educate, and provide a platform where YOU can be your unfiltered, unique self.

The magicians that will share with you their sparkles are experts in their field, renowned artists and shining stars each in their own way WHY " YOU " ?

As a revolutionary concept and platform, you will find You, the place for you to show off your sparkles and shine though art, content creation, acting, self development and empowernment.

In here, we create opportunities and believe in celebrating individuality, free from external standards.

We look forward to getting to know you, helping you show off your inner talent, and supporting your journey
