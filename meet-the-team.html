MEET THE
FOUNDER

Rita Samaha
an artist, model, and dancer from Beirut. Since 2017, she’s been working in the the art scene and fashion industry partnering with international brands . Each project is a catalyst for inspiration and influence. Her work, rooted in diverse experiences and intensive training, aims to empower Arab women through art. Explore her website to witness my journey, and if intrigued, feel free to reach out. Welcome to her realm where art fuels purpose and each creative endeavor paves the way for positive change.

Coming from the Middle East, it was hard to reach any goal or achieve any dream, as she was alone navigating this challenging industry. The scene lacked any community of empowerment or support for young arab women looking to shine in the field. She had to knock on doors alone, face challenges and obstacles. This is why she decided to create this platform, to raise the voice of arab talents and potentials, share opportunities, show them the road and walk it with them. You is a community of support, where you can meet people that paved the way in the industry, people that found keys and the knowledge on the road ready to give them to you so you open your own doors.

Here, nothing is unreachable, it is only YOU, the Unreachable Star.

Lea Keyrouz

Creative Director

Lea is a visionary creative director whose journey is deeply rooted in her academic background in art. With a solid foundation in artistic principles and techniques from her studies, she brings a profound understanding of visual aesthetics to her role. Her education has profoundly influenced her unique approach, seamlessly blending artistic principles with strategic thinking.

Lea sees everywhere a fusion of creativity, imagination, and a passion for the visual world. This perspective drives her to create designs that are both visually stunning and conceptually rich.

For her, each project is a chance to turn ideas into art that connects, resonates, and leaves a lasting impression.

For Lea, every person is a living work of art, and her objective is to produce content that captures and showcases this distinctive essence.

Patrick Mecherkany

Photographer & Videographer

Born and based in Beirut. His life experiences and situations deeply influenced his work.

His entry into photography was heartfelt. Inspired by his father, who always carried a camera wherever he went, Patrick felt a pull towards photography, especially after his father's passing in 2015. Using the very camera that once belonged to his dad, he started capturing moments that resonated with his emotions and perspectives.

He's collaborated with well-known local brands, Patrick always ensures his unique touch shines through. He also found a special connection with the unseen world, blending it effortlessly into his pictures. He has a knack for seeing beyond the obvious, aiming to present familiar subjects in new and refreshing ways.
